  • Singing

    Ephesians 5:19

    Our singing at Twin Creeks consists of us singing with our voices alone "making melody with our hearts to the Lord" (Eph. 5:19). The result is an uplifting experience, filling the auditorium with praise to our Father and Creator!

  • Prayer

    Phillipians 4:6

    We will approach the Father's throne with our petitions, with our praise, and with thanksgiving for everything that He has done for us. As we draw nearer to God, He draws nearer to us (James 4:8).

  • Communion

    1 Corinthians 11:26

    We will remember Jesus's death and resurrection every Sunday as we see the example given from the first century church in Acts 20:7. We will have a brief talk before we hand out the Lord's Supper to prepare our minds to proclaim the Lord's death.

  • Studying

    2 Timothy 2:15

    We are dedicated to following God's Word exactly as He has outlined in the Holy Scriptures. Join us to grow your faith and understanding of the most influential book the world has ever seen!
