Our Assembly

We conduct our worship according to the pattern we find in the New Testament. We sing with our voices unaccompanied, we pray to our Father, we proclaim the Lord's death and resurrection through communion, we give as the Lord blesses us, and we study God's Word to show ourselves approved as workers rightly dividing the truth.

Come Join Us!

We pray that you will find us to be a group of people who honor God by following His Word. We do not follow man made traditions nor are we a part of any human organization. We have one head, Jesus Christ (Colossians 1:18). We believe that all things pertaining to life and godliness have been revealed to man in the Word of God (2 Peter 1:3). And so we search the Scriptures daily, and we invite you to join us!
